Naman Vidya School

Welcome to Naman Vidya School

Naman Vidya School was founded in the year 2005 by Naman Vidya, which is a non-profit-making society registered under the Societies Registration Act (1860). Naman Vidya is a CBSE Affiliated Co-ed, Day-Cum-Boarding School having Nursery, Prep, Classes I to X. Teaching is conducted in English. World class residential facilities are available for boys at the school.
