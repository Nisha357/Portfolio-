INTER SCIENCE COLLEGE, HAZARIBAGH





Established in year 2005, INTER SCIENCE COLLEGE HAZARIBAG is located in Rural area of Jharkhand state/ut of India. In Ward No.01 area of Hazaribagh block of Hazaribag district. Area pincode is 825301.

School is providing Jr. College level education and is being managed by Private Unaided Organisation.

Medium of instruction is Hindi language and school is Co-educational.
