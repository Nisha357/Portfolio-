Welcome to DAV School HAZARIBAG

The entire educational process here revolves around guiding the students and providing them with all the relevant inputs to enablen them to take on the real world of work with consummate ease. The school is co-educational and affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi, upto class XII. Although the medium of instruction is English, Hindi and Sanskrit are also given equal importance. Computer education is compulsory from class IV onwards to ensure that our students fit the requirements of the fast-changing world. The school has earned accolades from one and all for maintaining a standard par excellence in academics, sports and co-curricular activities.
